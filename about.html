ABOUT ME

I am Assistant Director for Fellowships at The Citadel in Charleston, South Carolina. I coordinate The Citadel's Project GO grant, which funds intensive Mandarin immersion programs in Taiwan. I'm a 2013 graduate of the University of Texas at Austin's Lyndon B. Johnson School of Public Affairs, a 2010 Truman Scholar, and a student in the University of South Carolina's Doctorate of Education in Curriculum and Instruction program.
